About Us

Suzhou Skyler Bearing Co., Ltd. was founded in 2006, is situated in Suzhou Xiangcheng Ave 168, is specialized in producing metric tapered roller bearings, Tapered roller bearings, double row tapered roller bearings, deep groove ball bearings, cylindrical roller bearings, spherical roller bearings.

Production Specifications outer diameter Φ15mm-Φ220mm, registered trademarks for "Schuyler" card, code-named "ZSKL". Testing equipment company, you can outline the shape of the product, roundness, roughness, vibration, noise, cracks, dynamic and non-destructive testing, ISO9001: 2000 quality system is working effectively, SGS certification, to ensure that the whole process of product quality control.

Products sold throughout the country and exported to the United States, Europe, the Middle East and Southeast Asian countries and regions, products widely used in motors, automobiles, motorcycles, mining, oil, and so on. We are willing to provide foreign customers with quality products and excellent service!
